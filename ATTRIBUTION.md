# Media Attributions

This page provides attribution for all photos and videos used on tdrealtyohio.com. All media is sourced from legal, freely available sources with proper licensing.

## About Our Media

All images and videos on this website are sourced exclusively from **official APIs** that provide legally reusable content:

- **[Pexels](https://www.pexels.com)** - Licensed under the [Pexels License](https://www.pexels.com/license/)
- **[Pixabay](https://pixabay.com)** - Licensed under the [Pixabay License](https://pixabay.com/service/license/)
- **[Unsplash](https://unsplash.com)** - Licensed under the [Unsplash License](https://unsplash.com/license)
- **[Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org)** - Various Creative Commons and public domain licenses

**We do not scrape or use unlicensed content.** All media passes through automated license verification before being used on the site.

## Attributions by Source

### Pexels

Photos and videos from Pexels are provided by talented photographers and videographers around the world. While Pexels does not require attribution, we provide it to credit the creators:

- **License:** [Pexels License](https://www.pexels.com/license/)
- **Usage:** Free for personal and commercial use
- **Attribution:** Not required, but provided

### Pixabay

Pixabay provides high-quality images and videos released under the Pixabay License:

- **License:** [Pixabay License](https://pixabay.com/service/license/)
- **Usage:** Free for personal and commercial use
- **Attribution:** Not required, but provided

### Unsplash

Unsplash photos are contributed by professional photographers and require attribution:

- **License:** [Unsplash License](https://unsplash.com/license)
- **Usage:** Free for personal and commercial use
- **Attribution:** Required (always displayed)

### Wikimedia Commons

Media from Wikimedia Commons is used only when licensed under reuse-friendly licenses such as:

- **CC0 (Public Domain)** - No attribution required
- **CC-BY** (Creative Commons Attribution) - Attribution required
- **CC-BY-SA** (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike) - Attribution required
- **Public Domain** - No attribution required

We always provide attribution even when not required.

## How We Ensure License Compliance

Our automated media pipeline includes **6 verification gates** to ensure all media is properly licensed:

1. **License Verification** - Every asset must have valid license information
2. **Content Safety** - Rejects watermarked or logo-containing media
3. **Quality Check** - Ensures high-resolution, professional-grade media
4. **Deduplication** - Prevents duplicate content across sources
5. **Performance Optimization** - Optimizes for fast loading
6. **Build Validation** - Verifies all files and links before deployment
