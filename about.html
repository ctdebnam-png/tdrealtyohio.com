How Low Commission Works

A common question: how can TD Realty Ohio offer 1-2% commissions while others charge 3%?

Technology

Modern tools and systems streamline paperwork, communication, and marketing, reducing overhead without reducing service quality.

Efficiency

Focused operations and direct broker involvement on every transaction mean less overhead and more value passed to clients.

Volume

Lower rates attract more clients. More transactions at lower margins can be more sustainable than fewer transactions at higher margins.

The real estate industry has traditionally operated on commission structures established decades ago. Technology and modern business practices make it possible to deliver the same service for less. TD Realty Ohio passes those savings to clients.
