A Different Kind of Brokerage

TD Realty Ohio is a Central Ohio real estate brokerage that operates on a simple premise: the traditional commission model doesn't make sense anymore for a lot of people.

The 3% listing commission became standard when agents had to advertise in newspapers, host open houses to attract buyers, and spend significant time and money marketing each listing. Today, MLS syndication pushes listings to Zillow, Realtor.com, and every major real estate website automatically. The marketing costs that justified 3% have largely disappeared, but the fees haven't.

TD Realty Ohio charges less because the business model is more efficient. Lower overhead, fewer layers of management, no franchise fees going to a corporate headquarters. Those savings go directly to clients and agents.

For Sellers

Homeowners selling through TD Realty Ohio pay 1% listing commission when they sell and buy with the brokerage, or 2% for sell-only transactions. Every listing includes a free pre-listing inspection—a $400-500 value that helps sellers price accurately and avoid surprises during buyer inspections. Full MLS exposure, professional photography, expert negotiation, and closing support are standard.

For First-Time Buyers

First-time homebuyers working with TD Realty Ohio receive 1% of the purchase price back at closing. On a $400,000 home, that's $4,000 that can go toward closing costs, buying down the interest rate, or covering move-in expenses. Full buyer representation is included.

For Agents

TD Realty Ohio offers agents 100% commission on listings and 90% on buyer sides. No desk fees, no franchise fees, no monthly minimums. The brokerage provides broker support, MLS access, and E&O coverage while letting agents keep more of what they earn.

How Low Commission Works

A common question: how can TD Realty Ohio offer 1-2% commissions while others charge 3%?

Technology

Modern tools and systems streamline paperwork, communication, and marketing, reducing overhead without reducing service quality.

Efficiency

Focused operations and direct broker involvement on every transaction mean less overhead and more value passed to clients.

Volume

Lower rates attract more clients. More transactions at lower margins can be more sustainable than fewer transactions at higher margins.

The real estate industry has traditionally operated on commission structures established decades ago. Technology and modern business practices make it possible to deliver the same service for less. TD Realty Ohio passes those savings to clients.
