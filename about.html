About TD Realty Ohio

Full-service real estate with transparent pricing and a commitment to our clients.

Travis Debnam (License #2023006467)

I founded TD Realty Ohio with a simple idea: homeowners deserve full-service real estate representation without paying traditional commission rates. After years in the industry, I saw too many sellers paying 3% or more when technology and efficient operations make it possible to deliver the same excellent service for less.

I've been helping Central Ohio homeowners buy and sell real estate since 2017. In 2023, I became a licensed broker and founded TD Realty Ohio to put my client-first philosophy into practice. My approach is straightforward: transparent pricing, honest advice, and the kind of service that builds relationships rather than just closes transactions.

When you work with TD Realty Ohio, you work directly with me. I'm not a team leader passing you off to junior agents. I handle every aspect of your transaction personally, from initial consultation through closing.
