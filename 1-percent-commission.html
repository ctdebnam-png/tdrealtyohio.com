Frequently Asked Questions

How can you offer 1% commission when other agents charge 3%?
We've built an efficient brokerage with lower overhead. No fancy office, no bloated staff. We pass those savings directly to sellers while providing full-service representation.
What's included with the 1% listing fee?
Everything a traditional agent provides: MLS listing, professional photography, marketing, showing coordination, contract negotiation, and support through closing. Plus a free pre-listing home inspection.
Who qualifies for the 1% rate?
Sellers who are also buying their next home with TD Realty Ohio qualify for 1% listing commission. Sell-only clients pay 2%, which is still significantly less than the traditional 3%.
Is there a catch or hidden fees?
No hidden fees. The 1% or 2% is the full listing commission. Buyer agent commission is separate and negotiated based on your market and situation.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all of Central Ohio including Columbus, Westerville, Dublin, Powell, Delaware, Gahanna, New Albany, Hilliard, Grove City, Pickerington, Reynoldsburg, Upper Arlington, Worthington, and surrounding communities.
