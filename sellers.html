How the 1% and 2% Listing Fees Work

1% Listing Fee (Buy and Sell)

The 1% listing commission applies when you list your current home and purchase your next home with TD Realty Ohio. This path is designed for sellers who are also buyers and want to maximize savings across both transactions. The 1% fee applies to the listing side only.

2% Listing Fee (Sell Only)

The 2% listing commission applies if you are only selling and not purchasing your next home with TD Realty Ohio. This includes sellers who are relocating out of the area, moving into a rental, or purchasing through a different brokerage. The 2% fee still provides substantial savings compared to traditional 3% listing fees.

What's Included in Your Listing

Pre Listing Inspection

A pre listing inspection is included with every listing so you can make informed decisions about pricing, repairs, and disclosures before your home goes on the market. This reduces surprises during buyer negotiations.

Full MLS Listing

Your home is listed on the Columbus MLS and syndicated to major real estate platforms including Zillow, Realtor.com, and Redfin. Professional photography and accurate property details are included.

Pricing and Marketing Strategy

We provide comparable sales analysis, pricing recommendations, and a marketing plan based on your timeline and property condition. The goal is to position your home for competitive offers.

Showings and Open Houses

We coordinate showing requests, provide lockbox access for buyer agents, and can schedule open houses if requested. Showing feedback is tracked and provided to you regularly.

Offer Review and Negotiation

We review all offers with you, explain terms and contingencies, and handle negotiations on price, closing dates, repairs, and other contract items. You make final decisions on all offers.

Transaction Management to Closing

We manage the contract timeline, coordinate with title companies and lenders, track contingencies, and ensure all deadlines are met through closing. You receive regular updates throughout the process.

Typical Selling Timeline

1

Consultation and Inspection

Request a free consultation to confirm which fee path applies and schedule your pre listing inspection. The inspection typically takes 2-3 hours and the report is provided within 24 hours.

2

Pricing and Prep

Review inspection results and identify any prep items. We provide comparable sales analysis and pricing recommendations based on your timeline and property condition.

3

MLS Listing and Showings

Your home is listed on the Columbus MLS with professional photos. Showings typically begin within 24-48 hours of going live. We track showing feedback and adjust strategy if needed.

4

Offers and Negotiation

We review all offers with you and handle negotiations on price, closing dates, repairs, and other terms. Once you accept an offer, we enter the contract period.

5

Contract to Closing

We manage the buyer inspection period, appraisal, financing contingencies, and any repair negotiations. Most contracts close within 30-45 days of acceptance.
