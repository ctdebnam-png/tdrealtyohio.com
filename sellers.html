The Selling Process

From initial consultation to closing day, here's what to expect when selling with TD Realty Ohio.

Free Consultation

We'll discuss your timeline, confirm which fee path applies to your situation, and identify any preparation items to maximize your home's appeal and value.

Pre-Listing Inspection

A licensed inspector examines your property before listing. The report identifies condition issues so pricing and negotiations are based on known facts rather than surprises.

Listing and Marketing

Professional photography, MLS listing, and online syndication to Zillow, Realtor.com, and all major real estate platforms. Your home gets maximum exposure to qualified buyers.

Offers and Negotiation

Review all offers together and handle negotiations on price, closing dates, repairs, and other terms. Expert guidance to get you the best possible outcome.

Contract to Closing

Manage the buyer's inspection period, appraisal, financing contingencies, and any repair negotiations. Most contracts close within 30-45 days of acceptance.
