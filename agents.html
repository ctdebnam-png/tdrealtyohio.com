The Math Is Simple

TD Realty Ohio is a low-overhead brokerage in Central Ohio. We don't have expensive office space, franchise fees to pay up the chain, or layers of management taking a cut. Those savings go directly to our agents.

On a $400,000 listing at 2.5% commission ($10,000 gross), here's how the numbers compare:

Brokerage Model Agent Keeps
Traditional 70/30 split $7,000
Traditional 80/20 split $8,000
TD Realty Ohio (100%) $10,000

On buyer sides at 2.5% ($10,000 gross), TD Realty agents keep $9,000 compared to $8,000 at an 80/20 brokerage. That's $1,000 more per transaction.

Do 12 transactions a year and the difference adds up to thousands you're currently handing to your brokerage.

What TD Realty Doesn't Do

We don't micromanage your business. You're an independent contractor running your own operation. We don't tell you how to find clients, when to work, or what to wear.

We don't take a huge cut. The 100%/90% split is straightforward. No caps to hit, no complicated tier systems, no hidden fees that show up later.

We don't charge fees for everything. No desk fees. No technology fees. No transaction fees. No monthly minimums. The split is the split.

We're not a franchise. There's no corporate headquarters in another state taking money out of Central Ohio. TD Realty is a local brokerage serving the Columbus metro area.

Is This Right for You?

TD Realty Ohio works well for agents who are self-starters. If you need extensive training, daily office presence, or a team environment, a traditional brokerage might be a better fit.

But if you know how to run your business—or you're ready to learn by doing—and you want to keep more of what you earn, let's talk.

Whether you're an experienced agent tired of handing money to your current brokerage, or a new agent looking to get started without the overhead, the math works in your favor at TD Realty Ohio.
