What Is a 1% Commission Realtor?

A 1% commission realtor is a licensed real estate agent or broker who charges only 1% of your home's final sale price as their listing fee. This is significantly less than the traditional 2.5-3% that most listing agents in Columbus charge.

To put this in perspective:

  • Traditional 3% listing commission on a $400,000 home = $12,000
  • 1% listing commission on the same home = $4,000
  • Your savings = $8,000

That $8,000 stays in your pocket instead of going to your listing agent. And if you're buying your next home through the same 1% brokerage (like TD Realty Ohio), you could save even more through buyer rebates and incentives.

How Is This Different from Flat-Fee MLS?

It's important to understand the difference between a 1% commission realtor and flat-fee MLS services:

Flat-Fee MLS Services: You pay a fixed fee (often $300-$500) just to get your home listed on the MLS. After that, you're on your own for photos, showings, negotiations, and paperwork. This is essentially a DIY approach that can be risky for inexperienced sellers.

1% Commission Realtors: You get a licensed professional handling your entire transaction—from pricing strategy and professional photography to negotiating offers and coordinating closing. The only difference from traditional agents is the commission rate.
