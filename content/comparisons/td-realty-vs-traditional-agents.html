Addressing Common Concerns About Discount Realtors

"You get what you pay for"

The concern: Lower commission must mean lower quality service.

The reality: This assumes commission rate equals service quality. It doesn't. TD Realty provides the same professional marketing, MLS access, and expert negotiation as traditional agents. The savings come from efficient operations, not cut corners.

In fact, our free pre-listing inspection provides protection that most traditional agents don't offer at any price.

"My home will sell for less"

The concern: Discount agents won't work as hard, resulting in a lower sale price.

The reality: Your home's sale price is determined by the market, not your agent's commission rate. Both discount and traditional agents:

  • List on the same MLS
  • Syndicate to the same websites
  • Reach the same buyer pool
  • Use the same comparable sales data for pricing

A well-priced home in good condition sells at market value regardless of whether the listing agent charges 1% or 3%.

"Buyer's agents won't show my home"

The concern: Agents will steer buyers away from discount-listed homes.

The reality: The listing commission (what you pay TD Realty) is separate from the buyer's agent commission. You can offer whatever buyer's agent commission you choose—typically 2-2.5% in Columbus.

Buyer's agents are compensated the same whether your listing agent charges 1% or 3%. They have no financial reason to avoid your listing.

"I won't get the same attention"

The concern: Discount agents take on too many clients and spread themselves thin.

The reality: At TD Realty Ohio, you work directly with Broker Travis Riddle. There's no hand-off to assistants or transaction coordinators. Every listing receives personal attention because our business model depends on satisfied clients and referrals.

"There must be hidden fees"

The concern: The low commission is offset by add-on charges.

The reality: TD Realty Ohio has no hidden fees. Our 1% (or 2% for sell-only) includes all standard services. No photography fees, no admin fees, no transaction fees. The quoted commission is your complete listing cost.

(Note: Some discount brokerages do charge extras—always ask for a complete fee breakdown before signing with any agent.)

When a Traditional Agent Might Make Sense

We believe discount realtors serve most sellers well. However, there are situations where a traditional agent might be worth the extra cost:

  • Complex situations: Estate sales, divorces, short sales, or properties with legal complications may benefit from agents who specialize in these areas.
  • Unique properties: Ultra-luxury homes, historic properties, or unusual properties might need specialized marketing networks.
  • Existing relationships: If you have a trusted agent who's helped you before, that relationship has value.
  • Specific expertise needs: Some sellers need agents with particular knowledge (commercial conversion, land development, etc.).

For the typical Columbus home sale, however, a full-service discount realtor provides equivalent service at significant savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do discount realtors provide the same service as traditional agents?

Full-service discount realtors like TD Realty Ohio provide the same services as traditional agents: professional photography, MLS listing, marketing, showings, negotiations, and closing coordination. The difference is the commission rate, not the service level. In fact, TD Realty includes a free pre-listing inspection that most traditional agents don't offer.

Will my home sell for less with a discount realtor?

No. Sale price is determined by market conditions, your home's condition, location, and pricing strategy—not by commission rate. A skilled discount realtor uses the same MLS, marketing channels, and negotiation tactics as traditional agents. Your home reaches the same buyers and sells at market value.

Why do some agents charge 3% if 1% is possible?

Traditional commission rates were established decades ago when marketing was expensive and labor-intensive. Technology has reduced many of these costs, but commission rates stayed the same at most brokerages. Discount brokerages pass these efficiency savings to clients while maintaining service quality.

Are there hidden fees with discount realtors?

Some discount services do charge extra for photography, lockboxes, or admin fees. TD Realty Ohio has no hidden fees—our quoted commission includes all standard services plus a free pre-listing inspection. Always ask any agent for a complete fee breakdown before signing.
