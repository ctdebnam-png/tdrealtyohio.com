Key Differences Explained

The Free Pre-Listing Inspection

This is the most significant differentiator. TD Realty Ohio includes a professional home inspection (normally $400-$500) with every listing at no additional cost.

Why this matters:

  • You discover issues before buyers do
  • You can fix problems on your timeline, with competitive quotes
  • You avoid surprise negotiation demands that cost thousands
  • You reduce the risk of deals falling through
  • You negotiate from a position of knowledge, not reaction

Homes That Click does not include this service. You would need to pay for a pre-listing inspection separately if you wanted one.

We believe the free pre-listing inspection often saves sellers more than its $400-$500 value through avoided negotiation losses and prevented deal failures. [LINK: pre-listing-inspection]

Commission Structure

TD Realty Ohio:

  • 1% listing commission when you buy AND sell with us
  • 2% listing commission for sell-only clients

Homes That Click:

  • 1% listing commission (verify current rates directly with them)

If you're only selling (not buying), Homes That Click may offer a lower base rate. If you're both selling your current home and buying your next one, TD Realty's 1% rate applies and you get the free pre-listing inspection.

First-Time Homebuyer Benefits

TD Realty Ohio offers 1% cashback to first-time homebuyers at closing. This is separate from seller services but relevant if you're helping family members purchase or if you're selling to buy your first home.

Who Should Choose Each Option?

Consider TD Realty Ohio If:

  • You want the security of a pre-listing inspection included
  • You're both buying and selling (1% rate applies)
  • You value working directly with the broker
  • You have a first-time buyer in your household
  • Your home might have issues you'd prefer to address proactively

Consider Homes That Click If:

  • You're only selling (not buying) and want the lowest possible rate
  • You prefer a company with a longer track record
  • You've already had a pre-listing inspection done
  • You know your home is in excellent condition with no surprises

Consider Either If:

  • You want to save thousands vs. traditional 3% agents
  • You want full-service representation (not flat-fee DIY)
  • You want a local Columbus professional, not a national call center

Making Your Decision

We believe the fairest approach is to talk to both companies. Ask:

  1. What's your exact commission rate for my situation?
  2. What services are included in that rate?
  3. Are there any additional fees?
  4. Who will I work with directly?
  5. What's your experience in my specific neighborhood?
  6. Can you provide references from recent clients?

Both TD Realty Ohio and Homes That Click offer free consultations. Take advantage of both to see which feels like the better fit for your needs.

Why We Include the Free Pre-Listing Inspection

At TD Realty Ohio, we've seen too many deals go sideways over inspection surprises. A buyer's inspector finds an issue, the buyer panics or demands excessive credits, and sellers end up paying far more than the repair would have cost.

The free pre-listing inspection is our way of protecting clients from this scenario. Yes, it costs us $400-$500 per listing. But it saves our clients money, reduces stress, and leads to smoother transactions.

We believe this protection is worth more than the commission difference between our services—especially if your home has deferred maintenance, is older, or if you simply want peace of mind.
