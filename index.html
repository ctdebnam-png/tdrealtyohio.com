First-time homebuyers

Get 1% Cash Back at Closing on Your First Home

First-time homebuyers who purchase a home in Central Ohio with TD Realty Ohio receive 1% of the purchase price back at closing. This cash back is in addition to the reduced listing commission if you're also selling a home. The 1% can be used for closing costs, moving expenses, or any other purpose.

How it works

When you purchase your first home with TD Realty Ohio, you receive 1% of the purchase price as a credit at closing. On a $300,000 purchase, that's $3,000 back at closing.

Who qualifies

Generally applies to buyers who have not owned a primary residence in the past 3 years. Confirm eligibility during your consultation.

Combine benefits

If you're selling and buying, you can list for 1% and receive 1% cash back on your purchase. Maximum savings on both sides.

What You Get with TD Realty Ohio

Clear Fee Structure

The listing fee is 1% when you list and purchase your next home with TD Realty Ohio, and 2% for sell only listings. This keeps the fee decision tied to your move plan instead of being negotiated late. You can request the fee path in writing during the consultation.

Pre Listing Inspection Included

The inspection is done before the home goes live so you can decide what to repair, what to disclose, and how to price. This supports stronger negotiations because unknowns are reduced before offers are received. It also reduces the likelihood of surprises after a buyer inspection.

Full MLS Service

Listing distribution and presentation determine showing volume and buyer quality. Your listing is positioned for MLS visibility and syndication workflows that buyers actually use. The goal is to increase offer quality rather than inflate traffic metrics.
