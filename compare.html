Why Traditional Commission Rates Are Outdated

The 6% total commission model (3% to listing agent, 3% to buyer's agent) was established decades ago when selling a home required expensive newspaper ads, physical brochures, and significant marketing overhead.

Today, technology has changed everything:

  • MLS syndication automatically distributes your listing to Zillow, Realtor.com, and hundreds of other sites
  • Digital photography has dramatically reduced marketing costs
  • Online showing scheduling streamlines the process
  • Electronic signatures speed up transactions

Yet many agents still charge 3% as if it's 1995. TD Realty Ohio has built an efficient, technology-enabled brokerage that delivers the same results for less. We pass those savings directly to you.
