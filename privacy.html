Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

TD Realty Ohio, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out a contact form on our website
  • Request information about our services
  • Engage us as your real estate agent
  • Communicate with us via phone, email, or other methods

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, property address, and other details relevant to your real estate transaction.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Facilitate real estate transactions on your behalf
  • Send you information about properties or market updates (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services and website

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Other parties to a real estate transaction (buyers, sellers, other agents) as necessary
  • Service providers who assist with our business operations
  • Government agencies as required by law
  • MLS systems as required for listing properties

Website Analytics

Our website may use analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our site. This data is used to improve our website and services.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@tdrealtyohio.com.
