Fair Housing Policy

TD Realty Ohio, LLC is committed to compliance with all federal, state, and local fair housing laws. We do not discriminate against any person because of race, color, religion, sex, handicap, familial status, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, military status, ancestry, or any other protected class under federal, state, or local law.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing equal professional service to all persons in the purchase, sale, rental, or financing of housing. We will not:

  • Refuse to sell, rent, or negotiate with any person
  • Discriminate in the terms, conditions, or privileges of housing
  • Engage in blockbusting or steering
  • Deny membership or participation in any multiple listing service or real estate organization
  • Discriminate in advertising or marketing

Federal Fair Housing Act

The Fair Housing Act prohibits discrimination in housing based on:

  • Race
  • Color
  • National Origin
  • Religion
  • Sex
  • Familial Status
  • Disability

Ohio Civil Rights Act

Ohio law provides additional protections against housing discrimination based on:

  • Ancestry
  • Military Status

Filing a Complaint

If you believe you have been discriminated against in a housing transaction, you may file a complaint with:

  • U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD): 1-800-669-9777
  • Ohio Civil Rights Commission: 1-888-278-7101
