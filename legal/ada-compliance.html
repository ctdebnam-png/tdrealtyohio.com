Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

TD Realty Ohio, LLC is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Sufficient color contrast for text readability
  • Descriptive labels for form fields
  • Responsive design for various devices and screen sizes
  • Skip navigation links

Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us.

Alternative Formats

If you need information from our website in an alternative format, please contact us and we will work with you to provide the information in a way that meets your needs.
