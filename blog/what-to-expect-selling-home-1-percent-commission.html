What to Expect When Selling Your Home for 1% Commission

By Travis Debnam, Licensed Ohio Real Estate Broker | Updated January 2026

Considering a 1% commission realtor for your home sale? Here's exactly what to expect when working with TD Realty Ohio.

The Initial Consultation

Your first meeting covers:

  • Your goals: Timeline, where you're moving, any constraints
  • Pricing strategy: Comparative market analysis of similar Columbus sales
  • Commission explanation: Clear breakdown of costs with no hidden fees
  • Process overview: What happens from listing to closing

This consultation is free with no obligation. You'll leave knowing exactly what your home might sell for and what you'll net after costs.

Listing Agreement

Once you decide to proceed, you'll sign a listing agreement specifying:

  • Commission rate: 1% (if buying and selling) or 2% (sell only)
  • Listing duration (typically 6 months)
  • Services included
  • Any offered buyer agent compensation

Pre-Listing Preparation

What happens before your home goes live:

Pre-Listing Inspection

TD Realty Ohio schedules a professional home inspection. You'll receive a detailed report of your home's condition—the same comprehensive inspection buyers typically order. This is included at no extra cost.

Professional Photography

High-quality photos are scheduled. This isn't smartphone shots—it's professional real estate photography that showcases your home on Zillow, Realtor.com, and the Columbus MLS.

Listing Creation

Your broker creates compelling listing descriptions highlighting your home's best features and the local area.

Active Marketing

Once listed, your home receives:

  • MLS exposure: Listed on Columbus REALTORS® MLS
  • Syndication: Automatic distribution to Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, and hundreds of other sites
  • Online advertising: Targeted digital marketing
  • Showing coordination: All requests managed professionally

Showings and Feedback

Your broker coordinates all showings and communicates feedback. You'll know what buyers think and can adjust strategy if needed.

Offer Negotiation

When offers come in, your broker:

  • Reviews all terms—not just price
  • Explains strengths and weaknesses of each offer
  • Negotiates on your behalf
  • Helps you make informed decisions

Contract to Close

After accepting an offer:

  • Contract management and deadline tracking
  • Coordination with buyer's agent, lender, and title company
  • Inspection navigation (much smoother with pre-listing inspection)
  • Problem-solving if issues arise
  • Final walkthrough and closing coordination

What's Different From Traditional Agents?

Honestly? Not much in terms of service. The key differences:

  • Cost: You pay significantly less commission
  • Direct access: You work with the broker, not assistants
  • Pre-listing inspection: Most traditional agents don't include this
  • No frills: We focus on what sells homes, not flashy but ineffective marketing

The Bottom Line

Selling with a 1% commission agent like TD Realty Ohio means getting full professional service while keeping more of your equity. The process is the same—only the cost to you is different.
