Columbus Real Estate Commission Rates: Complete Guide

How commission is typically structured and what to ask before you sign.

Commission and compensation can vary by transaction and are negotiable. When evaluating representation, focus on:

  • Services included (marketing, photos, MLS exposure, negotiation, transaction management)
  • Fee structure and what conditions apply
  • Communication and who handles the work
