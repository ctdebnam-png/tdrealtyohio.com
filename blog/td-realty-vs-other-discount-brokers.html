Types of Discount Real Estate Services

Flat-Fee MLS Listing ($300-$500)

What you get: Your home listed on the MLS for a one-time fee. That's it.

What you don't get: Photography, pricing guidance, marketing, showing coordination, negotiation, or transaction management. You do everything yourself.

Best for: Experienced sellers comfortable handling all aspects of the sale.

Limited-Service Discount Brokers (1-2%)

What you get: Basic listing services with limited support. Often staffed by call centers or overwhelmed agents handling high volume.

What you don't get: Personal attention, local expertise, or someone invested in your specific transaction.

Best for: Sellers who need some support but are comfortable handling most issues themselves.

What you get: Full-service representation at a reduced rate. Licensed broker handling your transaction personally. Pre-listing inspection included.

What you don't get: Excessive commission charges.

Best for: Sellers who want professional service without overpaying.
