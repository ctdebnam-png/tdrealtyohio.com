How Much Can You Save Selling Your Columbus Home for 1% Commission?

By Travis Debnam | January 15, 2026

If you're planning to sell your home in Columbus, Ohio, one of the biggest expenses you'll face is the real estate commission. Traditionally, home sellers pay around 3% of the sale price to their listing agent. But what if you could cut that cost by two-thirds?

At TD Realty Ohio, we offer full-service listing representation for just 1-2% commission, depending on whether you're also buying a home. In this article, I'll break down exactly how much you can save and what you get for that lower rate.

The Math: 1% vs 3% Commission on Columbus Homes

Let's start with the numbers. The listing agent commission is calculated as a percentage of your home's sale price. Here's how 1% compares to the traditional 3%:

Example: $350,000 home (approximate Columbus median)

  • Traditional 3% listing commission: $10,500
  • TD Realty Ohio 1% commission: $3,500
  • Your savings: $7,000

That's $7,000 more equity in your pocket. For many Columbus families, that's a significant chunk of a down payment on their next home, moving expenses, or simply money saved for other priorities.

A lower commission doesn't mean less service. Here's what every TD Realty Ohio seller receives:

Your home is listed on the Columbus REALTORS MLS and automatically syndicated to Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, Trulia, and hundreds of other real estate websites. This gives your listing the same exposure as any traditionally-priced listing.

Professional Photography

High-quality photos are essential for attracting buyers online. Professional photography is included with every listing to showcase your home at its best.

Showing Coordination

I coordinate all showings, working with your schedule to make the process convenient. You don't have to field calls from agents or manage appointments yourself.

Expert Negotiation

When offers come in, I handle all negotiations on your behalf. My goal is to get you the best possible price and terms. This includes negotiating repairs after inspection, managing multiple offers, and navigating counteroffers.

Contract to Close Management

From accepted offer to closing day, I manage every detail: coordinating with the title company, monitoring deadlines, ensuring contingencies are met, and troubleshooting any issues that arise.

Free Pre-Listing Home Inspection

This is a significant value-add that most agents don't offer. Before listing your home, I arrange for a licensed inspector to examine the property. This inspection, typically valued at $400-500, is included at no additional cost to you.

The pre-listing inspection helps you understand your home's condition before buyers do. You can choose to make repairs, adjust your pricing, or disclose issues upfront. Either way, you avoid the surprise of a buyer's inspection derailing your sale at the last minute.

Why a Pre-Listing Inspection Is Valuable

Many Columbus home sales hit snags during the buyer's inspection period. The buyer discovers issues, requests thousands in repairs or credits, and suddenly your proceeds are reduced far more than any commission savings.

A pre-listing inspection gives you the power of knowledge. You can:

  • Fix issues before listing - Address problems on your timeline, get competitive bids, and maintain control
  • Price accurately - Factor known issues into your asking price rather than negotiating down later
  • Reduce negotiation surprises - Buyers have fewer opportunities to renegotiate when you've already addressed or disclosed issues
  • Speed up closing - Fewer inspection-related delays means a smoother path to the closing table

This free inspection alone can save you more than the commission difference, making TD Realty Ohio's 1-2% rate even more valuable.

What About the Buyer's Agent Commission?
