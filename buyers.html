First-Time Buyer? Get 1% Back at Closing

Full buyer representation with cash back. On a $300,000 home, that's $3,000 returned to you at closing.

How the Cash Back Program Works

When you're a first-time homebuyer working with TD Realty Ohio, you receive 1% of the purchase price back at closing. This comes from the buyer's agent commission, so it costs you nothing extra.

Example on a $300,000 Home:

  • Buyer broker commission (3%) $9,000
  • Cash back to you (1%) $3,000
  • TD Realty Ohio keeps $6,000

The cash back can be used toward closing costs, prepaid items, or any other purpose allowed by your lender.

Buying and Selling? Save Even More

If you're purchasing a home AND selling your current home with TD Realty Ohio, you get the best of both programs:

  • 1% listing commission on your current home sale (instead of 2%)
  • Full buyer representation on your purchase
  • Coordinated timing between both transactions
  • One agent managing the entire process

This combination maximizes your savings on both sides of the transaction.

Important Information About Buyer Representation

Compensation for buyer representation is negotiable and not set by law or any real estate board or MLS. Before you begin working with a buyer's agent, you'll receive a clear explanation of services and any associated costs.

The cash back program is subject to lender approval and may be applied toward closing costs or prepaid items. Some loan programs have restrictions on buyer rebates. We'll discuss your specific situation during our initial consultation.
