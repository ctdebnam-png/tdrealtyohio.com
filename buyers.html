Common Questions

You receive 1 percent of the purchase price back at closing. For example, on a $300,000 home, you get $3,000 cash back at closing. This applies to your first home purchase and can be used for moving costs, furniture, repairs, or any other expense.

Buyer agent compensation varies by transaction. We'll discuss the specifics of how buyer representation works in the current market during our initial consultation. Compensation is negotiable and not set by law or any real estate board or MLS.

If you're selling your current home AND purchasing your next home with TD Realty Ohio, you list your current home for just 1 percent commission instead of 2 percent. This applies whether you buy first and sell, or sell first and buy.

We serve the Central Ohio area including Columbus, Westerville, Worthington, Dublin, Powell, Delaware, Gahanna, New Albany, Hilliard, Upper Arlington, and surrounding communities.

Buyer Broker Compensation: Compensation for buyer representation is negotiable and not set by law or any real estate board or MLS. Before you begin working with a buyer's agent, you'll receive a clear explanation of services and any associated costs. We're happy to discuss compensation options during your initial consultation.
