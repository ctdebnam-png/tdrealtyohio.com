Common Questions

Buyer agent compensation varies by transaction. We'll discuss the specifics of how buyer representation works in the current market during our initial consultation.

If you're selling your current home AND purchasing your next home with TD Realty Ohio, you list your current home for just 1% commission instead of the standard rate. This applies whether you buy first and sell, or sell first and buy.

The timeline varies based on market conditions and your specific situation. Some buyers find a home in weeks, others take months. Once under contract, closing typically takes 30-45 days for financed purchases.

We serve the Central Ohio area including Columbus, Westerville, Worthington, Dublin, Powell, Delaware, Gahanna, New Albany, Hilliard, Upper Arlington, and surrounding communities.

Buyer Broker Compensation: Compensation for buyer representation is negotiable and not set by law or any real estate board or MLS. Before you begin working with a buyer's agent, you'll receive a clear explanation of services and any associated costs. We're happy to discuss compensation options during your initial consultation.
