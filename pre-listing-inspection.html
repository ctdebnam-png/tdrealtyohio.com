Pre-Listing Inspection

Know your home's condition before you list. Price accurately, avoid surprises, and close faster.

Why Pre-Listing Inspections Matter

The inspection is often the biggest pinch point in real estate transactions. We eliminate that stress upfront.

Accurate Pricing

Know exactly what condition your home is in so you can price it accurately from day one. No guessing, no surprises.

Fewer Surprises

When buyers do their inspection, there are no unexpected issues that derail the transaction or lead to last-minute renegotiations.

Faster Closings

Transactions move more smoothly when everyone knows the home's condition upfront. Less back-and-forth means faster closings.

Stronger Negotiating Position

Disclose known issues upfront and price accordingly. Buyers can't use inspection findings as leverage for last-minute price reductions.

Fix or Disclose

You choose whether to repair issues before listing or disclose them and adjust your price. Either way, you're in control.

Included at No Extra Cost

The pre-listing inspection is included with every TD Realty Ohio listing. It's part of our full-service approach.

What's Inspected

A comprehensive look at your home's major systems and components.

Structure

Foundation, framing, walls, ceilings, floors, and overall structural integrity.

Roof and Exterior

Roofing materials, gutters, siding, trim, windows, doors, and drainage.

Electrical

Service panel, wiring, outlets, switches, and overall electrical safety.

Plumbing

Water supply, drain lines, water heater, fixtures, and water pressure.

HVAC

Heating and cooling systems, ductwork, thermostats, and ventilation.

Interior

Walls, ceilings, floors, stairs, railings, and built-in appliances.

How It Works

1

Schedule the Inspection

After you decide to list with TD Realty Ohio, we schedule a licensed home inspector to evaluate your property.

2

Inspection Day

The inspector thoroughly examines your home's major systems and components, typically taking 2-3 hours.

3

Review the Report

You receive a detailed report with findings. We review it together and discuss your options.

4

Make a Decision

Choose to repair issues before listing, adjust your price to account for them, or simply disclose and let buyers decide.

5

List with Confidence

Your home goes on the market with full transparency. No surprises, no last-minute renegotiations.
