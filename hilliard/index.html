About Hilliard, Ohio

City Overview

Hilliard is a city of approximately 37,000 residents located northwest of Columbus in Franklin County. Originally a small railroad town, Hilliard has grown into one of Central Ohio's most desirable suburbs. The city offers a blend of established neighborhoods with mature trees and newer developments with modern amenities. Easy access to I-270 and downtown Columbus makes Hilliard popular with commuters.

Old Hilliard, the historic downtown area, features local shops, restaurants, and community events including the popular First Fridays series. The city has invested significantly in parks and recreation, with extensive trails and community facilities.

Neighborhoods and Schools

Hilliard offers diverse neighborhoods from the historic areas near Old Hilliard to newer planned communities. Popular areas include Hilliard Crossing, Tanglewood, and areas near Station Park. Many neighborhoods feature walking paths, community pools, and easy access to city parks.

Hilliard City Schools is one of the largest districts in Central Ohio with three comprehensive high schools and consistently strong academic and athletic programs. The district's size allows for diverse course offerings and extracurricular activities.

Landmarks and Attractions

Old Hilliard serves as the community's gathering place with local shops, restaurants, and events. The Heritage Rail Trail connects Hilliard to other communities via a multi-use path popular with cyclists and walkers. Hilliard's Recreation and Parks Department operates numerous parks including Homestead Park, Roger A. Reynolds Municipal Park, and the Hilliard Community Center with fitness facilities and programs. The annual Festival of the Arts and summer concert series draw residents together throughout the year.

Hilliard Real Estate Market

Market Conditions

Hilliard's real estate market benefits from strong schools, convenient location, and family-friendly amenities. The city attracts buyers seeking value compared to some other northwest suburbs while maintaining excellent quality of life. Well-priced homes typically sell within a reasonable timeframe with steady buyer demand throughout the year.

Home Types and Pricing

Hilliard offers a variety of housing options from ranch homes and colonials in established neighborhoods to newer two-story homes in planned communities. Pricing ranges from starter homes in the mid-$200,000s to executive homes exceeding $600,000. The pre-listing inspection we include helps ensure accurate pricing based on your home's actual condition.
