Fair Housing Statement

Equal Housing Opportunity

TD Realty Ohio is committed to compliance with all federal, state, and local fair housing laws. We are dedicated to upholding the Fair Housing Act which makes it illegal to discriminate in housing based on:

  • Race or color
  • National origin
  • Religion
  • Sex (including gender identity and sexual orientation)
  • Familial status
  • Disability

Ohio law additionally prohibits discrimination based on ancestry and military status.

Our Commitment

TD Realty Ohio pledges to:

  • Provide equal professional service to all clients regardless of protected class
  • Show and offer all available properties without regard to protected characteristics
  • Never make housing unavailable or deny services based on protected class
  • Refuse to participate in any discriminatory practices in the real estate industry
  • Support and participate in fair housing training and education

If You Experience Discrimination

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination, you may file a complaint with:
