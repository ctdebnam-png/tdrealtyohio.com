Voice and Tone Guidelines for TD Realty Ohio

STYLE:
- Straightforward and professional
- No hype or exaggeration
- Friendly but not overly casual
- Informative without being preachy
- Confident but humble

MUST AVOID:
- No guarantees or promises about outcomes
- No invented or unverified statistics
- No "best," "fastest," "top," "leading," "premier," or similar superlatives
- No steering language that implies certain areas are better for certain groups
- No protected-class language (race, color, religion, sex, national origin, familial status, disability)
- No pressure tactics or urgency language ("Act now!", "Don't miss out!", "Won't last!")
- No disparaging comments about other agents, brokerages, or areas
- No claims about market performance without verified data

LANGUAGE:
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Avoid jargon that buyers/sellers may not understand
- Be inclusive in all references to potential clients
- Focus on factual property features and community amenities
