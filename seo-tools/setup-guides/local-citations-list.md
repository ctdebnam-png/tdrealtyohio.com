# Local Citations List for TD Realty Ohio

Citations = Your business listed on directories with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone).

**Critical for Local SEO:** Google uses citations to verify your business and determine local rankings.

---

## NAP Consistency Requirements

**Use EXACTLY this format everywhere:**

```
Business Name: TD Realty Ohio, LLC
Address: Westerville, OH 43081  (or full street address if you want)
Phone: (614) 956-8656
Website: https://tdrealtyohio.com
Email: travis@tdrealtyohio.com
```

**Important:** 
- Use EXACT same format on every directory
- Same abbreviations (St vs Street, OH vs Ohio)
- Same phone format
- Inconsistency hurts rankings!

## Priority 2: Google Business Profile

**Most Important Citation - Do This First!**

1. **Google Business Profile** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - URL: business.google.com
   - Impact: CRITICAL - Appears in Google Maps, Local Pack
   - Time: 20 minutes + verification
   - Cost: FREE

### Setup Steps:

1. Go to business.google.com
2. Search for "TD Realty Ohio" (might already exist)
3. If exists: Claim it
4. If not: Create new listing
5. Fill out completely:
   - Business name
   - Address
   - Phone
   - Website
   - Hours
   - Services (Real Estate Agent, Home Sellers, Home Buyers)
   - Description (use keywords)
   - Photos (10+ high quality)
6. Verify via postcard (takes 5-7 days)

### After Verification:

- Post weekly updates (market news, new listings)
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Add more photos monthly
- Use Google Posts feature
- Monitor insights weekly

```
Would you mind sharing your experience? Your feedback helps other Central Ohio homeowners discover our services.

Google: [Google review link]
Zillow: [Zillow review link]
Facebook: [Facebook review link]

Thank you!
Travis Debnam
(614) 956-8656
```

2. **Make it easy:**
   - Send direct review links (not just website)
   - Follow up once after 1 week if no review
   - Thank them when they leave a review

3. **Respond to every review:**
   - Good reviews: Thank them, mention specific detail
   - Bad reviews: Professional, solution-focused, take offline
