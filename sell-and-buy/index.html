Common Sell + Buy Scenarios

Upsizing

Growing family needs more space. You'll sell your starter home and purchase something larger, often in a nearby suburb with better schools.

Common timing: Find the new home first, then list your current home with a sale contingency or coordinate closings.

Downsizing

Kids moved out, retiring, or just want less maintenance. You'll sell the larger home and move to something more manageable.

Common timing: Sell first to know your exact budget, then purchase with the flexibility of being a cash or well-financed buyer.

Relocating Within the Metro

Job change, lifestyle preference, or wanting to be closer to family. Moving from one Central Ohio community to another.

Common timing: Depends on lease terms, school schedules, or job start dates. We'll build a plan around your constraints.

Investment Transition

Selling an investment property and using the proceeds to purchase a primary residence, or vice versa.

Common timing: Often driven by 1031 exchange timelines or market conditions. Requires careful coordination.
