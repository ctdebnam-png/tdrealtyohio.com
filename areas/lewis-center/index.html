Real Estate in Lewis Center, Ohio

Lewis Center is one of the most sought-after communities in Delaware County, known for its excellent schools, upscale housing, and prime location near the Polaris area. This unincorporated community has experienced tremendous growth over the past two decades, transforming from rural farmland into a thriving suburban destination that attracts families seeking top-tier education and modern amenities.

The real estate market in Lewis Center commands premium prices, with a median home price around $475,000 reflecting the area's desirability. Homes here tend to be newer construction, typically built within the last 20 years, featuring open floor plans, modern kitchens, and generous lot sizes. Popular subdivisions offer a range of options from quality single-family homes to luxury estates with resort-style amenities.

Location is a major selling point for Lewis Center properties. Residents enjoy immediate access to Polaris Fashion Place and its surrounding retail and dining options, while maintaining a quieter suburban atmosphere. The short commute to downtown Columbus via I-71 or US-23 makes Lewis Center ideal for professionals working in the city. Whether you're selling a move-up home in Olentangy Falls or a luxury property in Powell Woods, TD Realty Ohio can help you capture maximum value while paying significantly less in commission.

Schools in Lewis Center

Lewis Center is served by Olentangy Local Schools, consistently ranked among the best school districts in Ohio. The district is known for academic excellence, championship athletic programs, and outstanding fine arts. Olentangy schools regularly achieve top scores on state assessments and send graduates to prestigious universities nationwide.
