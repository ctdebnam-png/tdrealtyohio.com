Real Estate in Columbus, Ohio

Columbus is Ohio's capital and largest city, known for its diverse economy, world-class universities, and vibrant neighborhoods. The Columbus real estate market offers opportunities for first-time buyers, growing families, and investors alike.

From the historic homes of German Village to the modern condos of the Short North, Columbus neighborhoods each have their own character. The city's strong job market, driven by healthcare, education, technology, and finance sectors, continues to attract new residents.

TD Realty Ohio provides full-service real estate in Columbus at reduced commission rates. Whether you're buying your first home or selling to upgrade, you get professional representation while keeping more of your equity.

Popular Columbus Neighborhoods

  • Short North - Arts district with galleries, restaurants, and nightlife
  • German Village - Historic brick homes and tree-lined streets
  • Grandview Heights - Walkable community near downtown
  • Clintonville - Family-friendly with parks and local shops
  • Italian Village - Urban living with Victorian architecture
  • Victorian Village - Historic district near downtown
