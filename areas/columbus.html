Schools in Columbus

Columbus City Schools is one of Ohio's largest districts, serving the majority of the city. However, Columbus has unique school boundaries where some neighborhoods are served by suburban districts despite being within city limits.

District Diversity: Parts of Columbus fall within Westerville, Gahanna-Jefferson, South-Western, Groveport Madison, and other suburban districts. The "win-win" areas offer Columbus addresses with suburban schools. Always verify district assignments.

Notable options: Columbus City Schools offers magnet programs including Fort Hayes Arts & Academic High School, Centennial High School, and STEM programs. Private and charter school options are abundant throughout the city.

Neighborhoods in Columbus

Columbus offers incredibly diverse neighborhoods, from historic urban enclaves to family-friendly suburban pockets within city limits.

Short North Arts District

Columbus's trendiest neighborhood with galleries, boutiques, and restaurants along High Street. Mix of renovated Victorians and modern condos. Walkable to downtown and Arena District.

$300K - $800K Very Walkable Urban

German Village

One of the largest privately-funded historic districts in the US. Brick streets, restored 1800s homes, and the famous Book Loft. Strict architectural standards maintain character.

$400K - $900K Historic Walkable

Clintonville

Established tree-lined streets with early 1900s homes. Strong community identity, local shops along High Street, and easy access to Whetstone Park. Popular with families and professionals.

$350K - $600K No HOA Family-Friendly

Grandview Heights

Small city within Columbus known for excellent schools and walkable downtown. Character homes from the 1920s-40s. Technically a separate municipality with its own services.

$400K - $700K Own Schools Walkable

Victorian Village

Beautiful Victorian homes near downtown and the Short North. Mix of single-family homes, duplexes, and small apartment buildings. Strong preservation efforts maintain historic character.

$350K - $750K Historic Near OSU

Bexley

Affluent inner-ring suburb with top-rated schools and stately homes. Tree-lined streets, Capital University, and strong community events. Technically a separate city.

$400K - $1M+ Own Schools Established
