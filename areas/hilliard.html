Schools in Hilliard

Hilliard City Schools operates three high schools: Hilliard Davidson, Hilliard Darby, and Hilliard Bradley. The district is known for balanced academics, strong athletics, and comprehensive career-technical programs.

Well-Rounded District: Hilliard offers something for everyone with competitive athletics (Davidson is a perennial state contender), strong band and choir programs, and diverse academic tracks from college prep to career technical education.

Notable features: The district's partnership with Tolles Career & Technical Center provides unique career pathway opportunities. All three high schools maintain solid academic reputations with good college placement.

Hilliard is one of the fastest-selling markets in Central Ohio with an average of just 22 days on market. The combination of good schools, reasonable prices, and proximity to jobs on the west side keeps demand strong. Up 5.5% year-over-year, Hilliard offers relative value compared to northern suburbs.

Neighborhoods in Hilliard

Hilliard offers charming Old Hilliard living, established family neighborhoods, and newer construction at accessible prices.

Old Hilliard

The historic heart of Hilliard with tree-lined streets, character homes, and walkable access to the revitalized downtown. Mix of renovated older homes and selective infill construction.

$325K - $500K Walkable downtown Character homes

Brookside Woods

Established 1990s neighborhood with mature trees and well-maintained homes. Popular with families for its quiet streets, walking paths, and community pool.

$375K - $500K HOA Community pool

Hoffman Farms

Newer community (2010s) with contemporary floor plans and active HOA. Community events, pool, and well-maintained common areas. Popular with young families.

$400K - $550K Active HOA Modern homes

Tanglewood

Established 1980s subdivision with larger lots and mature landscaping. Mix of ranches and two-stories. No HOA gives homeowners flexibility. Close to Heritage Golf Club.

$300K - $425K No HOA Larger lots

Hilliard Crossing

Convenient location near shopping and I-270 access. Mix of home styles and ages. Great for commuters working downtown or on the west side.

$275K - $375K Easy commute Near shopping
