Real Estate in Blacklick, Ohio

Blacklick is one of the most desirable communities in the eastern Columbus suburbs, offering an exceptional combination of newer homes, natural beauty, excellent schools, and convenient access to shopping and employment centers. This unincorporated community in Franklin County has experienced significant growth over the past two decades, attracting families and professionals seeking quality housing in a park-like setting.

The Blacklick real estate market features predominantly newer construction, with many homes built within the last 15-20 years. With a median home price around $400,000, buyers find modern floor plans, energy-efficient construction, and well-planned neighborhoods with community amenities. The area offers everything from quality single-family homes to luxury properties, appealing to a range of buyers seeking move-up or executive housing.

What truly distinguishes Blacklick is its outstanding access to nature. Blacklick Woods Metro Park provides over 600 acres of forests, wetlands, and trails right in residents' backyards. The community also offers easy access to the Three Creeks Metro Park and the extensive Metro Parks trail system. Despite this natural setting, Blacklick residents are just minutes from Easton Town Center and New Albany shopping and dining. Whether you're selling a family home near the golf course or a newer property in one of the popular subdivisions, TD Realty Ohio can help you achieve maximum value while significantly reducing commission costs.

Schools in Blacklick

Blacklick is primarily served by Gahanna-Jefferson City Schools, a highly-regarded district known for academic excellence and strong extracurricular programs. The district's Lincoln High School consistently ranks among the top public high schools in Central Ohio. Some areas of Blacklick are also served by Columbus City Schools or other nearby districts depending on specific location.
