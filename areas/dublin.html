Real Estate in Dublin, Ohio

Dublin is one of Central Ohio's most desirable communities, known for its excellent Dublin City Schools, upscale neighborhoods, and strong sense of community. The city hosts the annual Dublin Irish Festival and features the renowned Muirfield Village Golf Club.

Dublin offers a mix of executive homes, newer developments, and established neighborhoods. The Bridge Street District provides a walkable urban environment, while areas like Tartan Fields and Ballantrae offer golf course living.
