Real Estate in Powell, Ohio

Powell is one of Central Ohio's fastest-growing and most desirable communities, known for its exceptional schools, family-friendly atmosphere, and high quality of life. Located in Delaware County just north of Columbus, Powell and the surrounding Liberty Township area have experienced tremendous growth over the past two decades. The city combines small-town charm with modern amenities, featuring a walkable historic downtown, extensive park system, and numerous community events throughout the year.

The Powell real estate market is characterized by newer construction, spacious lots, and family-oriented neighborhoods. Home prices typically range from $400,000 for smaller homes to well over $1 million for luxury properties in premier communities. The median home price of approximately $550,000 reflects the area's strong demand driven by the nationally-recognized Olentangy Local Schools district. Many buyers specifically seek Powell addresses to access these top-rated schools, creating consistent demand across all price points.

Whether you're selling a townhome, a family home in one of the many subdivisions, or a custom-built property on acreage, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your proceeds with our low commission structure and targeted marketing approach. Our knowledge of the Powell market ensures your home reaches qualified buyers seeking what this exceptional community offers.

Schools in Powell

Olentangy Local Schools is consistently ranked among the top school districts in Ohio and the nation. The district serves over 23,000 students across multiple elementary schools, middle schools, and four high schools: Olentangy, Olentangy Liberty, Olentangy Orange, and Olentangy Berlin. The district's strong academics, extensive extracurricular offerings, and community support make it one of the primary reasons families move to Powell and surrounding areas.
