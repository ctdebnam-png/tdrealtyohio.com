Real Estate in Delaware, Ohio

Delaware is a charming county seat with rich history and a vibrant small-town atmosphere, located just 30 minutes north of downtown Columbus. Home to Ohio Wesleyan University since 1842, Delaware blends academic culture with community warmth. The city of approximately 42,000 residents offers a unique combination of historic architecture, tree-lined streets, and a growing economy that attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike.

The Delaware real estate market features diverse housing options, from beautifully preserved Victorian homes near downtown to new construction in expanding subdivisions on the city's edges. The historic downtown area, centered around Sandusky Street, offers walkable access to local shops, restaurants, and community events like the Little Brown Jug harness race and the Delaware Arts Festival. As Delaware County continues to be one of Ohio's fastest-growing counties, property values have shown steady appreciation.

Whether you're selling a historic home near Ohio Wesleyan, a family home in one of the established neighborhoods, or a newer property in a growing subdivision, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your proceeds with our low commission structure. The median home price of approximately $350,000 offers excellent value for buyers seeking small-town living with easy Columbus access.

Schools in Delaware

Delaware City Schools serves the community with a strong commitment to academic excellence and student development. The district operates one high school (Delaware Hayes), one middle school, and several elementary schools. Known for its dedicated teachers and comprehensive programs, Delaware City Schools consistently produces graduates who go on to success in higher education and careers. The district also benefits from proximity to Ohio Wesleyan University, creating educational partnership opportunities.
