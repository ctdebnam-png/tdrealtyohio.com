Real Estate in Grove City, Ohio

Grove City is one of the most affordable and family-friendly suburbs in the Columbus metropolitan area, offering excellent value for homebuyers seeking quality housing, good schools, and a convenient location. Located just southwest of downtown Columbus, Grove City provides easy access to major employers, shopping, and entertainment while maintaining its own distinct community identity centered around the charming Grove City Town Center.

The housing market in Grove City features a diverse mix of properties, from well-maintained ranch homes built in the 1960s and 70s to newer construction in expanding subdivisions. With a median home price around $300,000, Grove City attracts first-time homebuyers, young families, and anyone looking for more square footage without stretching their budget. The city has experienced steady growth while maintaining the friendly, small-town atmosphere that residents love.

Grove City's strategic location offers quick commutes to downtown Columbus, Rickenbacker International Airport, and the growing commercial corridor along I-71. The Town Center district hosts community events throughout the year, including the popular Arts in the Alley festival. Whether you're selling a starter home near Broadway or a newer property in the Pinnacle Club area, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your sale price while minimizing commission costs.

Grove City Neighborhoods

  • Town Center - Historic downtown area with local shops, restaurants, and community events
  • Pinnacle Club - Newer golf course community with upscale homes
  • Hoover Crossing - Family-friendly subdivision with good access to amenities
  • Grant Run - Established neighborhood with mature trees and larger lots
  • Buckeye Woods - Popular subdivision near parks and schools
  • Windsor Park - Well-maintained community with active HOA

Schools in Grove City

Grove City is served primarily by South-Western City Schools, one of the largest school districts in Ohio. The district operates multiple elementary schools, middle schools, and Grove City High School. South-Western City Schools offers a comprehensive curriculum, extracurricular activities, and career-technical programs that prepare students for college and careers.
