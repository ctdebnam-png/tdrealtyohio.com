Discover Worthington

Worthington is a historic city known for its New England-style village green, well-preserved architecture, and strong sense of community. Old Worthington features tree-lined streets, local shops, and dining options that draw residents and visitors to the heart of the city.

The city hosts year-round events including the Worthington Farmers Market, outdoor concerts at the Village Green, and seasonal celebrations. The Worthington Libraries system offers extensive programs and resources, while parks and recreation facilities provide opportunities for outdoor activities and community gatherings.

With excellent schools, diverse housing options, and proximity to Columbus, Worthington appeals to families and professionals seeking an established community with strong civic pride and quality amenities.
