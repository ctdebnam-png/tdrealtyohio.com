Real Estate in New Albany, Ohio

New Albany is Central Ohio's premier planned community, renowned for its Georgian architectural style, exceptional schools, and country club lifestyle. Founded in 1991 with a vision to create an intentionally designed community, New Albany has grown into one of the most prestigious addresses in the Columbus metropolitan area. The city features meticulously maintained streetscapes, extensive walking trails, and the renowned New Albany Country Club. Home to executives, entrepreneurs, and professionals, New Albany offers an unmatched quality of life with its blend of refined living and community engagement.

The New Albany real estate market is characterized by high-end homes with strict architectural guidelines that maintain the community's cohesive Georgian aesthetic. Home prices typically range from $450,000 for smaller properties to well over $2 million for estates in the most exclusive areas. The median home price of approximately $600,000 reflects the premium nature of this community. The nationally-recognized New Albany-Plain Local Schools district is consistently ranked among Ohio's best, driving significant demand from families seeking academic excellence. Additionally, the New Albany International Business Park has attracted major employers including Meta, Google, and Intel investments, further strengthening property values.

Whether you're selling a village home near Market Square, a property in one of the golf communities, or a custom estate, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your proceeds with our low commission structure. On higher-value New Albany homes, our commission savings can be substantial - often $10,000 to $20,000 or more compared to traditional agents.

Schools in New Albany

New Albany-Plain Local Schools is consistently ranked among the top school districts in Ohio and nationally. The district is known for its innovative approach to education, including the Learning Campus that brings together all schools on a connected campus. With a focus on academic excellence, character development, and community service, New Albany schools attract families from throughout Central Ohio. The district's reputation is a primary driver of real estate demand and property values.
