Real Estate in Westerville, Ohio

Westerville is one of Central Ohio's most sought-after communities, known for its excellent schools, charming Uptown district, and strong sense of community. With a population of approximately 40,000, Westerville offers the perfect balance of suburban living with easy access to Columbus. The city earned its nickname "The Dry Capital of the World" due to its historical ties to the Anti-Saloon League, and today maintains that community-focused heritage through numerous festivals, events, and local businesses.

The Westerville real estate market features diverse housing options ranging from historic homes near Uptown and Otterbein University to newer construction in developments throughout the city. Home prices typically range from $250,000 for starter homes to over $700,000 for executive properties. The median home price of around $375,000 reflects the area's desirability and strong school district. Properties in walkable Uptown areas and near top-rated schools often command premium prices and sell quickly.

Whether you're selling a condo near Otterbein, a family home in the Westerville City Schools district, or a larger property in one of the newer developments, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your proceeds with our low commission structure and local expertise. As Westerville-based agents, we know this community inside and out.

Schools in Westerville

Westerville City Schools is one of the highest-rated districts in Central Ohio, serving over 15,000 students across multiple elementary schools, middle schools, and three high schools: Westerville North, Westerville Central, and Westerville South. The district is known for strong academics, competitive athletics, and excellent fine arts programs. The quality of schools is a major driver of real estate demand in Westerville.
