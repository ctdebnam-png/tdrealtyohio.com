Real Estate in Pataskala, Ohio

Pataskala is one of the fastest-growing communities in the eastern Columbus suburbs, attracting buyers who want more home for their money without sacrificing access to employment centers and amenities. Located in Licking County just east of Columbus, Pataskala offers an appealing combination of affordable housing, new construction, and a community that still maintains its friendly, small-town roots.

The Pataskala real estate market stands out for its value proposition. With a median home price around $325,000, buyers can find newer homes with modern features, spacious yards, and quality construction at prices significantly below comparable homes in western Columbus suburbs. The area has seen substantial new development in recent years, with builders offering a range of floor plans and price points to meet various buyer needs.

Pataskala's location provides convenient access to Columbus via I-70 and State Route 16, making it practical for commuters working downtown or in the eastern business corridor. The area also benefits from proximity to major retail and dining options, while maintaining a more relaxed pace of life. Whether you're selling an established home near downtown Pataskala or a newer property in one of the growing subdivisions, TD Realty Ohio can help you navigate the market and maximize your sale price while minimizing costs.

Schools in Pataskala

Pataskala is served primarily by two school districts: Licking Heights Local Schools and Southwest Licking Local Schools. Licking Heights has experienced significant growth and investment in facilities and programs, while Southwest Licking serves portions of the community with its own strong academic programs. Both districts offer comprehensive education from elementary through high school.
