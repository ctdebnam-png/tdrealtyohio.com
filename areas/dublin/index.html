Real Estate in Dublin, Ohio

Dublin is one of Central Ohio's premier communities, renowned for its top-rated schools, thriving business environment, and exceptional quality of life. Home to approximately 50,000 residents, Dublin consistently ranks among the best places to live in Ohio. The city hosts major corporate headquarters including Cardinal Health and Wendy's, while the emerging tech corridor along US-33 continues to attract innovative companies and high-income professionals to the area.

The Dublin real estate market offers tremendous diversity, from luxury estates in Muirfield Village and Tartan Fields to modern condos and apartments in the vibrant Bridge Park mixed-use development. Home prices range from $300,000 for smaller homes and condos to well over $1 million for premium properties. The median home price of approximately $500,000 reflects Dublin's status as one of Central Ohio's most desirable addresses. The highly-rated Dublin City Schools district drives significant demand, particularly for homes in established neighborhoods.

Whether you're selling a townhome near Bridge Park, a family home in the Dublin schools district, or a luxury property in one of the golf course communities, TD Realty Ohio can help you maximize your proceeds with our low commission structure and comprehensive marketing approach. Our expertise in the Central Ohio market ensures your Dublin home reaches the right buyers.

  • Tartan Fields - Upscale golf course community with luxury homes
  • Ballantrae - Established neighborhood with larger lots and mature trees
  • Riviera - Family-friendly neighborhood with excellent schools access

Schools in Dublin

Dublin City Schools is one of the highest-performing districts in Ohio, consistently earning excellent ratings from the Ohio Department of Education. The district serves over 16,000 students across multiple elementary schools, three middle schools, and three high schools: Dublin Coffman, Dublin Jerome, and Dublin Scioto. Strong academics, competitive athletics, and award-winning fine arts programs make Dublin schools a major attraction for families.
