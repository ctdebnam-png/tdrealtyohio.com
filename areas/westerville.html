Real Estate in Westerville, Ohio

Westerville is consistently ranked among the best places to live in Ohio. Known as "The Dry Capital of the World" for its historic prohibition stance, today Westerville offers excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and a charming uptown district.

The Westerville City Schools are highly rated, making the city a top choice for families. Otterbein University adds a college-town atmosphere, while the Uptown area provides walkable shopping, dining, and entertainment.
