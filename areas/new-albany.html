Schools in New Albany

New Albany-Plain Local Schools is consistently ranked among Ohio's very best districts. The single-campus model houses all grades on one interconnected campus, creating a unique educational community.

Elite Academics: NAPLS regularly achieves top scores statewide. The district's focus on innovation, character development, and college preparation attracts families willing to pay premium home prices for access.

Notable features: State-of-the-art facilities, strong arts programs, and the unique "Learning Campus" design that encourages collaboration across grade levels. Exceptional college counseling and AP offerings.

Neighborhoods in New Albany

New Albany is a master-planned community with strict architectural standards, extensive green space, and cohesive Georgian-inspired design throughout.

New Albany Country Club

The crown jewel of New Albany with Jack Nicklaus-designed golf course, tennis, and social membership. Estate homes on large lots with premium finishes. Very exclusive.

$1M - $5M+ Country club Estate lots

The Links at New Albany

Golf course community with variety of home sizes. More accessible price point than Country Club while still offering golf and amenities. Popular with families.

$600K - $1.2M Golf community Various sizes

Keswick

Established village neighborhood with classic New Albany architecture. Tree-lined streets, walking distance to Market Square shops and restaurants. Strong community feel.

$550K - $900K Walkable Near Market Square

Fenway

Family-oriented neighborhood with community pool and active social calendar. Mix of colonials and transitional styles. Great for families with school-age children.

$600K - $950K Community pool Family-friendly

Lambton Park

Newer construction in the western expansion with modern floor plans. More entry-level pricing for New Albany while maintaining the community's architectural standards.

$500K - $800K New construction Modern plans
