Know Your Numbers Before You Shop

Before you start shopping, know your numbers. This calculator estimates your purchasing power based on standard lending guidelines.

How It Works

Lenders use two key ratios to determine how much you can borrow:

  • Front-end ratio (28%): Your monthly housing payment should not exceed 28% of your gross monthly income
  • Back-end ratio (36%): Your total monthly debts (including housing) should not exceed 36% of your gross monthly income

This calculator uses both ratios and gives you the more conservative estimate.

Note: These estimates are based on standard lending guidelines. Your actual purchasing power may vary based on credit score, loan type, and lender requirements. Property tax rates vary by county and school district in Central Ohio.
