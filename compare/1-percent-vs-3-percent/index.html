How Can the Commission Be Lower?

The 1% rate isn't about cutting corners. It's about running an efficient business:

Lower Overhead

No fancy office, no large staff, no expensive franchise fees. Operating costs are lower, so commission rates can be lower.

Technology Efficiency

Modern tools handle scheduling, paperwork, and communication more efficiently than traditional methods.

Combined Transactions

When you buy and sell together, the agent handles both sides. The combined business justifies the lower listing rate.

Direct Marketing

Referrals and direct outreach replace expensive traditional advertising and lead generation services.
