Commission & Pricing

Our 1% listing commission (available when you sell and buy with us) includes everything: full MLS listing, professional photography, marketing to Zillow/Realtor.com/Redfin, expert pricing guidance, negotiation support, contract management, and closing coordination. Plus, every listing includes a free pre-listing home inspection valued at $400-500.

On a $300,000 home, you save $6,000 with our 1% rate vs. a traditional 3% listing commission. With our 2% sell-only rate, you save $3,000. First-time buyers receive 1% cash back at closing—that's $3,000 on a $300,000 purchase.

Yes, our 1-2% rate covers the listing side only. You may choose to offer compensation to buyer's agents (typically 2-3%) to attract buyers with agent representation. We'll discuss the best strategy for your specific situation and local market conditions.

Our 1% listing commission applies when you both sell and buy with TD Realty Ohio. If you're only selling (not purchasing another home through us), the listing commission is 2%. Both rates include full service and the free pre-listing inspection.

Lower overhead, no franchise fees, efficient technology, and a volume-based business model. The traditional 3% commission was set when agents had to advertise in newspapers and spend significant money marketing listings. Today, MLS syndication automatically pushes listings everywhere. We pass those savings to you.

For Sellers

Every TD Realty Ohio listing includes a professional home inspection before you list—a $400-500 value at no extra cost. This helps you understand your home's condition, price accurately, make repairs proactively, and avoid surprises that can derail deals during buyer inspections.

In the Columbus metro area, well-priced homes typically sell within 10-20 days. Market conditions, pricing, location, and property condition all affect timing. Our free pre-listing inspection helps ensure your home is ready to sell quickly and for top dollar.

Yes, professional photography is included with every listing at no additional cost. Quality photos are essential for online marketing and attracting buyers. We also offer virtual tour options for properties that would benefit from them.

Our free pre-listing inspection identifies issues before you list. You can then decide whether to make repairs, adjust pricing, or disclose and sell as-is. Knowing about issues upfront prevents surprises and gives you control over how to handle them.

Yes, you can choose not to offer buyer agent compensation. However, most buyers work with agents, so this may reduce your buyer pool. We'll help you understand the trade-offs and make the best decision for your situation and local market.

For Buyers

First-time homebuyers who purchase with TD Realty Ohio receive 1% of the purchase price back at closing. On a $300,000 home, that's $3,000 you can use for closing costs, buying down your interest rate, or moving expenses. Full buyer representation is included.

The 1% cash back program is available to first-time homebuyers purchasing a primary residence in our service area. First-time buyer typically means you haven't owned a home in the past three years. Contact us to confirm eligibility for your situation.

Full buyer representation including property search assistance, showing coordination, market analysis, offer strategy, negotiation, inspection coordination, and support through closing. We help first-time buyers navigate every step of the home buying process.
